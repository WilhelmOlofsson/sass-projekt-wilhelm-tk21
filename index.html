Facts about Capys

Capybaras are the largest rodent living on earth. They live in the wild in South America, and are affectionately referred to as "Capys" by all their fans. It is estimated that Capys have 6.72 Billion fans all around the world with the rest simply never having had the pleasure of encountering one, whether it be in the wild, in a zoo or in images. It is factually and scientifically proven that it is not possible to not like a capybara.
